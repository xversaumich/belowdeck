### How to Play ###

> Please read the instructions on how to play below! Rules are subject to change.

>

>

> The player is pitted against a series of curated enemy encounters.

> Your means of Attack and Defence is your card deck.

> You and the enemy take turns.

> At the start of your turn, you draw 5 cards from your draw pile.

> Each card has an energy requirement to play. You start a turn with 3 energy, so you must be strategic in what cards you choose to play.

> At the end of your turn, all cards in your hand enter the discard pile. If you don't have enough cards in your draw pile at the start of the next turn, the discard pile is shuffled and then returned to the draw pile.

> You can see what action an enemy intends to take on their turn, denoted by a symbol floating above them, and a message in the terminal logs. This lets you plan out what cards to play during your turn.

> Your health and current status can be seen in the lower left corner, and the enemy's status above their portrait.

> If you reach 0 health, you lose. If the enemy reaches 0 health first, you win the round and move on to the next area.
